Temperature dependence ​

Arrhenius ​

The arrhenius type temperature dependence can be described by the following formula:

A(T)=Arefexp⁡EaF(1T−1Tref),

where, A is the concerning quantity. In BattMo this can be the electrode diffusion coefficient or reaction rate. Aref is the value of the quantity at the reference temperature, F is the Faraday constant, Ea is the activation energy, and T the temperature.
